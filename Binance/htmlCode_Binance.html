Meet the Team

Yi H. | Co-Founder

Yi is the Co-Founder of Binance and the Head of Binance Labs, where she leads the business, investment, marketing and branding strategy. Since Binance's launch in 2017, Yi led the business strategy, which saw the company successfully becoming one of the world's largest crypto exchanges within the span of six months. Yi also pivoted the company to a global blockchain ecosystem from just a crypto-to-crypto trading platform.

Yibo L. | Chief Business Officer

Yibo is the Chief Business Officer at Binance, where he leads the development and implementation of the company’s strategic plans to help progress the development of blockchain technologies. He oversees Binance Labs, M&A, JV, and strategic partnerships. Prior to joining Binance, Yibo held leadership roles in various global companies including Bird, Uber, and The Boston Consulting Group. He holds a PhD from MIT in EECS and earned a BA in English and BS in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Michigan.

Dana H. | Business Strategy & Ops

Dana provides direct support to Yi on Binance Labs, high impact projects, and other key initiatives within the company. Prior to joining Binance, Dana led product development and strategic investments at top notch tech companies, co-founded a startup and was also a macro strategist at Goldman Sachs. Dana holds an MBA degree from Stanford Graduate School of Business.

Andrew C. | M&A & Portfolio Management

Andrew leads Binance's strategic M&A integrations and our investment portfolio management efforts. Prior to Binance, he was a deal advisor specializing in financial services M&A in North America and Asia. Andrew is a CFA charterholder and a CPA, CA. He graduated from University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University with degrees in Mathematics and Business.

Alex O. | Investment

Alex is an Investment Director at Binance Labs and covers token, equity and strategic investments. Prior to Binance, Alex was an investment professional with a London-based technology fund for 2 years and an investment banker with Goldman Sachs for 4 years. He holds an MA degree in Econ and Maths from the University of St Andrews.

Dan F. | Investment
